About Me

Hi there, my name Joel Pereira and I am a freelance photographer. I do not think of pictures of a person or place as just a picture but more so a story that is being told about the person or place in the photograph. Which will leave you wanting to know more.
I have began my journey by purchasing a decent Canon camera that is used for photography which did cost a pretty penny, photography had started out as a hobby for me and then I developed a passion for it and now I am here today!
